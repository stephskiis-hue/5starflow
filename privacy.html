Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Who We Are

5StarFlow ("we," "us," or "our") provides automation services for home service businesses. We are based in Canada. You can contact us at hello@5starflow.com.

2. What Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly when you contact us or purchase a plan:

  • Your name and email address
  • Your business name and type
  • Your Google Business Profile link
  • Payment details (processed by a third-party payment processor — we do not store card numbers)

On the Fully Managed plan, we may also access your Jobber account (read-only, with your permission) and your email/SMS sending account in order to operate your automations.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To deliver the services you purchased
  • To communicate with you about your setup, support requests, or account
  • To send occasional tips or updates (you can opt out at any time)

We do not sell your information to anyone. We do not share your information with third parties except as necessary to operate the service (e.g., Zapier, email/SMS providers).

4. Your Clients' Information

As part of providing the review request service, we (or the automations we build) will process your clients' names and contact information to send review request messages on your behalf. We handle this data only as directed by you and only for the purpose of operating your automation. We do not store your clients' personal data beyond what is necessary to send the messages.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account information for as long as you are a customer. If you cancel your plan, we will delete or anonymize your data within 90 days upon request. Email hello@5starflow.com to make a deletion request.

6. Cookies

Our website does not use tracking cookies or analytics at this time. We may add basic analytics in the future and will update this policy accordingly.

7. Security

We use reasonable technical measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We encourage you to use strong, unique passwords for any accounts connected to our service.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal information at any time. Contact us at hello@5starflow.com and we will respond within 5 business days.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we'll notify active customers by email. The date at the top of this page will always reflect the most recent update.

10. Contact

Questions about this policy? Email us at hello@5starflow.com.
