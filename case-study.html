The Problem

He Was Doing Great Work. His Google Listing Didn't Show It.

Marcus had been running his lawn care business for four years. His clients loved him — return bookings were strong, word-of-mouth was steady. But when new customers Googled lawn care in Austin, they saw competitors with 80, 120, even 200 Google reviews. Marcus had 11.

"I knew I should be asking for reviews. I just never did it consistently. After a job I'm already driving to the next one. By the time I remembered, it felt weird to go back and ask."

"I had clients who would've happily left a review. I just never asked at the right time. 5StarFlow does the asking for me — automatically, every single time."

— Marcus D., Owner, Lawn Care Business, Austin TX
The Setup

Done in 2 Days. He Didn't Touch a Single Setting.

Marcus chose the Done For You plan. He filled out a short onboarding form — his business name, Google review link, and the tone he wanted his messages to have. Two days later, our team had everything connected and tested.

The automation runs inside his own Zapier account. When a Jobber invoice is marked paid, Zapier triggers the review request: first a text message, then a follow-up email a few hours later. Both are personalized with the client's first name and a direct link to Marcus's Google review page.

"I was skeptical it would actually work. Then I got my first new review the same day it went live. Someone I had done a job for three days before — they got the text, clicked the link, left a five-star review. I hadn't done anything."
